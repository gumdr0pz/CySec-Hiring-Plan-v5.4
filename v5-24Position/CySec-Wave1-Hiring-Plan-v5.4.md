## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Wave 1 is an 8-role, mixed-level foundation team anchored on PreSales.** v5.4 uses an 8-4-9 split with a clear seniority gradient: Wave 1 mixes 2 Leads + 5 Seniors + 1 Junior/Intermediate, Wave 2 is all Senior (revenue closers), and Wave 3 is all Junior/IC (scale-up bench).

The Professional Services Wave 1 seat is a **Technical Account Manager (Junior/Intermediate)** — the former Senior Professional Services Consultant has been removed from the structure.

### Key Changes from v5.3

| Metric | v5.3 (7-7-7) | v5.4 (8-4-9) | Change |
|--------|--------------|--------------|--------|
| Wave 1 Positions | 7 | 8 | +1 |
| Wave 1 Level Mix | Flat (Senior) | 2 Lead + 5 Senior + 1 Junior/Intermediate | Mixed |
| Wave 1 Focus | Sales + governance | PreSales-anchored | Re-anchored |
| Wave 2 Positions | 7 | 4 (all Senior) | -3 |
| Wave 3 Positions | 7 | 9 (all Junior/IC) | +2 |
| Wave 1 Cost (Min) | RM592,704 | RM653,184 | +RM60,480 |
| Wave 1 Cost (Max) | RM948,326 | RM1,045,128 | +RM96,802 |
| Total Cost (Min) | RM1,729,728 | RM1,536,192 | -RM193,536 |
| Total Cost (Max) | RM2,767,554 | RM2,457,960 | -RM309,594 |

**Moved into Wave 1:** Lead Researcher (OffSec) and CTI Lead (CTI) — to give the PreSales motion senior technical depth.
**Moved out of Wave 1:** Sales Specialist (Enterprise) → Wave 2 (sales closers follow the pipeline that PreSales builds).
**Replaced in Wave 1:** Senior PS Consultant → **Technical Account Manager (Junior/Intermediate)** in Professional Services.

**Rationale:**
- PreSales is the technical win-engine; anchoring Wave 1 on it lets the BU scope, demo, and win early pipeline before committing to dedicated sales hunters.
- Two technical Leads (OffSec, CTI) give presales demos and solution architectures real credibility.
- A Technical Account Manager establishes post-sale account management and recurring-revenue protection at a right-sized band.
- Total plan cost drops vs v5.3 because Wave 3 (9 Junior/IC) replaces higher-cost senior seats and the PS Wave 1 seat moves to the junior band.

### 3.1 Presales Consultant (Senior) — Sales ⭐ ANCHOR
- **Focus:** Technical presales, solution architecture, demos/POCs, RFP/RFI responses

- **Key Skills:** VoronCitadel platform, control frameworks (ISO 27001/NIST), regulatory requirements
- **Success:** GRC platform deployed, first customer onboarded
- **MRF:** MRF-002

### 3.4 Sr DevSecOps (Senior) — DevSecOps
- **Focus:** Security automation, CI/CD pipelines, cloud security
- **Key Skills:** CI/CD tools, cloud platforms, scripting (Python/Bash)
- **Success:** DevSecOps pipeline implemented, security monitoring established
- **MRF:** MRF-003

### 3.5 PM (Senior) — PM Team
- **Focus:** Project delivery, stakeholder coordination, process improvement
- **Key Skills:** PMP/PRINCE2, Agile/Scrum, stakeholder management
- **Success:** PM methodology established, first project portfolio
- **MRF:** MRF-004

### 3.6 Lead Researcher (Lead) — OffSec
- **Focus:** Offensive security research, exploit development, presales technical depth
- **Key Skills:** Vulnerability research, exploit dev, programming (Python/C/C++/ASM)
- **Success:** Research program established; technical capability briefings ready for presales
- **MRF:** MRF-006

### 3.7 CTI Lead (Lead) — CTI
- **Focus:** Threat intelligence operations, threat hunting, intelligence reporting
- **Key Skills:** CTI platforms (MISP, OpenCTI), GCTI, analytical writing
- **Success:** Threat intelligence platform operational; intel products support presales
- **MRF:** MRF-008

### 3.8 Technical Account Manager (Junior/Intermediate) — Professional Services
- **Focus:** Post-sale client relationships, delivery coordination, account health, retention/expansion
- **Key Skills:** Technical account management/customer success, cybersecurity/blockchain familiarity, client communication
- **Success:** Account-management framework and client health tracking established; high retention
- **MRF:** MRF-005
