### 1.3 Financial Position

The hiring plan is financially viable with costs ranging from 13.0% to 20.8% of the RM12M revenue target, well within the 25% revenue cap. ## 9. RECOMMENDATIONS

### 9.1 Financial Recommendations

1. **Target Minimum Salaries**: Hire at the lower end of ranges to maximize headroom — especially across the 8-role Wave 1.
2. **Phased Wave 1 Start Dates**: Stagger Wave 1 onboarding within the 90-day window to smooth the front-loaded cash outlay.
3. **Strict Wave Triggers**: Hold Waves 2 and 3 against RM4M+ SOWs and RM8M+ recurring revenue respectively.
4. **Performance Incentives**: Tie bonuses to revenue targets to align costs with income.

### 9.2 Budget Management

1. **Approval Gates**: Require approval before each wave trigger
2. **Cost Tracking**: Real-time cost tracking against budget
3. **Variance Analysis**: Monthly variance analysis with corrective actions
4. **Contingency Reserve**: Maintain 10-15% contingency for unexpected costs

### 9.3 Risk Mitigation

1. **Wave 1 Front-Load**: The 8-role Wave 1 commits RM677K–1,084K before revenue ramps — tie funding to board approval and manage with phased starts.
2. **Revenue Shortfall**: Prepare scale-down plan if revenue targets are not met.
3. **Cost Overruns**: Implement cost control measures and approval gates.
4. **Talent Market**: Monitor salary trends and adjust offers accordingly.
