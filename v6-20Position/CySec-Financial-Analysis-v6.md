## 9. RECOMMENDATIONS

1. **Operate within the 25% cap (15.9%–22.6% at base).** The total sits fully within 25% across the entire benchmark band — even the band ceiling is 22.6%; no cap relaxation required.
2. **Anchor offers to the Rate-card base benchmark** and sit toward the minimum of each band to hold the ratio well under 25% while remaining market-competitive.
3. **Strict Wave Triggers:** hold Waves 2 and 3 against RM4M+ SOWs and RM8M+ recurring revenue so the heavier L4-weighted waves only land as revenue materialises.
4. **General vs cyber-core discipline:** keep support roles general (dotted line), compartmentalise sensitive work, and handle any future spinoff by secondment.
